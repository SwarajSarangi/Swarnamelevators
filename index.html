About SWARNAM

Elevating Excellence Since 2022

SWARNAM is a leading manufacturer and installer of premium elevators, serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Our mission is to make vertical transportation safer, faster, and more comfortable.

We are experienced professionals redefining the elevator industry. With a focus on customized solutions, reliable local service, and strong partnerships, we stand out in the market. Though young, our team brings years of proven expertise in manufacturing, engineering, and sales, committed to supporting and growing with our partners while delivering service beyond products.

From compact home lifts to high-speed corporate solutions, we provide cutting-edge designs backed by 24/7 maintenance support.
